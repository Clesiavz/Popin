About

  • Who we are

    POP IN is professional Counsellor & Therapist Practise We aim to provide Effective, Confidential and Flexible counselling on the telephone or Online.

    Our experience includes working as therapists both in general mental health and addictions. In addition to this valuable experience, we have extensive training and are fully qualified to work in private practice.

  • What we offer

    We offer a confidential, non-judgemental and flexible approach to counselling. It is an opportunity for you to share, discover and, if you want, change.

    Our approach is based on working collaboratively with clients to help find their best way forward, our training includes a number of different therapeutic approaches, this allows us to tailor the sessions to best fit with clients and their current challenges.

  • Who we work with

    We work with individuals who have specific goals and change in mind and individuals who have a sense of “something” being wrong, but not knowing for certain what that “something” is. We work both short and long-term and offer several different ways of working, We have found this responds well to the uniqueness that we all present.

  • How to get started

    If you would like to know more or are considering moving forward with counselling a good way forward is an informal, no obligation chat on the phone, you can then ask any questions you may have, and we can see if what I can offer is of use.

    At this time, we are only able to offer Online Counselling and Group Therapy. We offer both daytime and evening appointments.

Are you coping with COVID 19

POP In have been your trusted and trustworthy mental health provider for many years, we realise that COVID 19 are causing added anxiety and stress for our clients, because of the social distancing regulations. Our motto is that our clients can POP In at any time, to speak to one of our counsellors or therapists.

We have decided to bring in new service to our clients and move to online counselling and group therapy on a secure platform to establish continued support to our clients in these dire times. Our research shows that there is little or no difference between the client experience in face to face session or online session via a secure online platform.

We aim to share relevant information about the transition via our Webpage through our new blogging service. The blog will serve as a guide to be in contact our counsellors and group therapist, as well as bring information about how to cope and manage mental health issues, like stress, loneliness and anxiety during this time. This will benefit our clients as it will be a more flexible service, which can fit around the busiest of schedules. There will be no travel costs or physical appointments to get to on time. Due to the change in service, we offer an assistance program in term of *discounted fee structure due to the financial strains that we face in our country currently because of COVID 19.

How is blog support beneficial

We have considered various other platforms like vlogging to share the needed information so that our clients can have a positive experience during the transition period moving to an online platform. We have decided in favour of blogging and not vlogging to create an optimum interaction with our clients.

We have found that online users, retain text information far better that audio, even if we think the we have gained information via audio, reading interaction is retained with more positive results within short-term memory. Because a blog can be instantly referenced, scanning can be quick and easy to pick out information. Whereas a vlog may have to be re-watched to be able to find specific information, may take longer and becomes time consuming.

Memorable images as well as colour contrast, along with eye catching headlines, are retained for a longer period in memory, than a vlog with similar images and audio. When complex or a huge amount of information needs to be shared, a blog is handy as the content can be split into different headings, which make it a lot easier for the user to consume. We strive to ensure that our information is credible, correct and valuable to our clients, thus editing grammatical errors in writing is far easier that editing a vlog, which needs special software and expertise that may be beyond our scope of experience at this time.

Is it safe and secure?

Please share your concerns on our contact page, or if you have any questions around privacy and security, and feel free to leave comments and suggestions.

The confidentiality of the online platforms and other social and digital interactions are in line with the APA The American Psychology Association guidelines, found on their page www.apa.org. Our counsellors and therapists are members of PsySSA, The South African Association for Counselling Psychology

As per ethical principles, we do not have clickbait on our page or use this blog for financial gain or entered in any other alliances with consumer products. We have measures in place to ensure that we protect the privacy of your information and will only record sessions upon written agreement.

How to ensure success

For a successful move into online counselling and group therapy with Pop In, keep checking our website and blog updates to keep you informed. By contacting us in the contact page, the counsellor or group therapist will be in touch, so that you can speak directly to us.

For online counselling and group therapy sessions you only need to connect through a secure line using, a computer, mobile, tablet or telephone When in session ensure all other applications are closed for your sessions. Privacy in terms of noise, comfortable seating, good lighting makes for a successful experience. Make sure that an alternative device is available if session is interrupted.

Most important take a break after the session, with a cup of tea.
